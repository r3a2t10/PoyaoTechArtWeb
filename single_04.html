So that I can Forget You

Unity / C# / VR / Audio Visualizer

2018 by Wu,Po-Yao


This project is an audio visualizer in VR form, presenting my favorite song “So That I Can Forget You” made by Astro Bunny. I get the spectrum and the beat of the music, making the objects in the VR scene moving and rotating. The objects in the VR scene are related to the lyrics, making the audience more immersed in the music and the VR 360 scene. I hope this work can present the beauty of the music in VR form.
